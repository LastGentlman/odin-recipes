Super Nachos

Ingredients

  • 36 chips, corn
  • 2 ball tomatoes, chopped and seeded
  • 1/4 cups fresh coriander
  • 1/4 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 tablespoons of cumin
  • 1 can of refried beans
  • 4 cups of Manchego cheese, or grated yellow
  • 6 pickled jalapeños, minced pickled

Preparation Metod

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees Celsius.
  2. In two baking trays, place the chips in a single layer.
  3. In two baking trays, place the chips in a single layer.
  4. In a deep bowl, mix the tomato, coriander and salt.
  5. In a skillet over medium heat, heat the oil and brown the onion, garlic, and cumin for 5 minutes.
  6. Pour the onion mixture over the tomato mixture and mix well.
  7. Put 1/2 tablespoon of beans on each chip and sprinkle cheese over all chips.
  8. Cook for 5 minutes or until all the cheese has melted.
  9. Remove from the oven, place on a plate and pour the tomato mixture with onion and jalapeños over them.

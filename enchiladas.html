Red Mexican Enchiladas

  • 148 gr old cheese, grated
  • 98 gr ancho chili peppers
  • 48 gr guajillo chili peppers
  • 16 tortillas
  • -1 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 0 cooked chicken breasts, shredded
  • 1 cooked potatoes, sliced
  • 1 cooked carrots, sliced
  • -1 chopped onion
  • Lard
  • Salt

    • Preparation Metod

    1. To prepare red enchiladas you have to toast the chilies, devein them and soak them in hot water; drain them when they soften and then grind them with the garlic cloves and a pinch of salt.
    2. Fry the resulting sauce in three tablespoons of hot butter and season with salt to taste.
    3. Add the broth and continue cooking until it becomes consistent.
    4. Moisten the tortillas in the chili and fry in plenty of hot butter.
    5. Fill them with cheese, breast, potatoes, carrots and chopped onion.
    6. Fold the tortillas in half and cover with more sauce; garnish with onion slices and cheese.
